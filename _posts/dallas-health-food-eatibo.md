## Are There Hidden Health Traps in Dallas's Favorite Foods?

Even in seemingly healthy choices, there can be hidden pitfalls. For instance, many common ingredients or preparation methods in local cuisine might surprise you. Think about hidden sugars in salad dressings or sauces, excessive amounts of seed oils in restaurant dishes, or even the high sodium content in seemingly innocent soups. Seed oils, while often marketed as healthy, can be inflammatory for some people when consumed in excess, especially when used in cooking at high temperatures. Without careful inspection, these can easily derail your health goals.
