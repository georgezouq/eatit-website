## When You're at Krieger's, What Are the Quick Checks for Truly Healthy Options?

Making quick, informed decisions in the store is key. Here are some actionable tips for on-the-spot evaluation:

*   **Scan the Ingredient List Length:** Generally, shorter ingredient lists with recognizable items are better. If it looks like a chemistry experiment, put it back.
*   **Prioritize Whole Foods:** Opt for unpackaged fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains as much as possible. These are inherently "clean."
*   **Sugar Watch:** Look for added sugars in all their forms (sucrose, glucose, dextrose, maltose, corn syrup, fruit juice concentrate). Aim for products with minimal or no added sugars.
*   **Healthy Fats vs. Unhealthy Fats:** Distinguish between beneficial fats (avocado, olive oil, nuts, seeds) and unhealthy ones (hydrogenated oils, soybean, corn, sunflower, safflower, and canola oils).
*   **Fiber and Protein:** These are your friends! Look for products rich in fiber (helps digestion and fullness) and protein (essential for muscle repair and satiety).
